## Interpretation

The TCP p95 smoke measurements are under the local targets of GET < 1 ms and SET
< 2 ms with AOF disabled for the latency benchmark. The SET p99 outlier crossed
1 ms during this run, so this does not claim sustained
throughput under disk-backed writes; AOF fsync policy must be benchmarked
separately for deployment-specific durability choices.

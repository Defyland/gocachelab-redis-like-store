# Personas

## Systems-Minded Backend Candidate

Needs to demonstrate networking, concurrency, persistence, and performance
without relying on a large external cache implementation.

Success means being able to explain lock scope, TTL lag, replay behavior, and
latency measurements from the repo.

## Tech Lead Reviewer

Needs to evaluate whether the author can make defensible engineering trade-offs.

Success means finding explicit alternatives, rejected complexity, failure tests,
and honest benchmark notes rather than unverified claims.

## Platform Engineer

Needs a compact reference for how a TCP daemon can expose operational surfaces.

Success means seeing where metrics, pprof, health checks, AOF recovery, and
runbooks fit into the service lifecycle.
